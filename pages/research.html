EZ-MD

A helping hand

A next-generation medical assistant robot designed for microscopic precision. Built in three adaptive models — Large, Medium and Small — EZ-MD enhances surgeon control, stability and accuracy across all surgical environments.

Meet the EZ-MD Family

EZ-MD Large

Engineered for full surgical theaters

Designed for high-complexity surgical suites, the EZ-MD Large delivers extended reach, multi-instrument control and superior micro-stability. Its reinforced actuator system maintains precision during long procedures where accuracy is critical.

EZ-MD Medium

The balance point between delicacy and reach

Built for mid-range procedures, the EZ-MD Medium excels in stitching, micro-cutting and tool guidance. Its hybrid arm structure allows for both delicate precision and the strength required in minimally invasive operations.

EZ-MD Small

Precision engineered within a millimeter

Optimized for internal, ultra-targeted surgical work, the EZ-MD Small operates where human hands cannot. With micro-level articulation and near-zero impact movement, it performs highly controlled maneuvers inside the smallest structures.

How EZ-MD Works

Sensing & Mapping

Micro-sensors create a living 3D map of tissue movement and structure.

Precision Stabilization

Real-time correction of micro-movements ensures surgical stability.

Adaptive Tooling

Tools automatically adjust to pressure, depth and angle requirements.
