ENTERTAINMENT

UNIT

EZ-EU:

The Entertainment Unit (EZ-EU) is a robot designed to entertain anyone, whatever they find entertaining. The robot is perfectly designed for: music, theatre, standup, childrens entertainment, magic and much more.

ENTERTAINMENT

MADE PERFECTION

What it does:
The robot scans people entering the entertainment-arena. The entertainment-arena contains several different stages, where all stages are hosted by an EZ-EU. By using its highly accurate facial-recognition-algorithm, the EZ-EU delegate the visitors to the stage which fit their interests for entertainment the most.

How it works:
The algorithm used by EZ-EU is designed to recognize their favourite form of entertainment. By using facial scanners, the algorithm can detect what kind of entertainment they like with high accuracy. Like said, EZ-EU can perform in most genres. They get delegated to their own stage where they perform the genre they are designated to.
