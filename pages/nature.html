DISCOVERY and
PRESERVATION

EasyCorp's Discovery & Preservation division leads the frontier of responsible exploration in 2125. Our mission is twofold: to uncover new knowledge across land, sea, and space while safeguarding the environments and histories we encounter. Through advanced autonomous systems—ranging from deep-ocean biomimetic explorers to eco-restoration units built from fully recycled components—we prioritize low-impact operations and long-term planetary care.

Focused on sustainable innovation, the division continues to develop technologies that enable deeper discovery with minimal disturbance. Whether mapping distant terrains, studying delicate ecosystems, or restoring natural habitats, Discovery & Preservation ensures that progress and protection move forward together.

Exploring Tomorrow,
Preserving What Matters Today.

EZ-SD: Precision Exploration for the Frontier Ahead.
Meet EZ-SD, EasyCorp's advanced spherical micro-rover built for the exploration challenges of 2125. Compact, autonomous, and highly durable, it navigates tight and delicate environments with zero-impact mobility.With quantum-mapping sensors, self-healing plating, and omni-directional propulsion, EZ-SD captures precise environmental data while protecting fragile ecosystems and structures.Whether surveying new worlds or preserving ancient sites, EZ-SD delivers a reliable, efficient, and future-ready solution—helping you discover more while disturbing less.

EZ-NP: Sustainable Design for a Living Future.
EZ-NP is EasyCorp's next-generation nature preservation unit, built entirely from recycled components and engineered to work in harmony with natural ecosystems. Using bio-sensitive scanners and adaptive ecological tools, it identifies soil and plant needs, restoring fragile environments with precise, low-impact care. Its modular functions—planting, pruning, hydrating, and stabilizing—allow EZ-NP to support landscapes without disturbing wildlife. As one of EasyCorp's most public-friendly designs, it stands as a symbol of sustainable, circular engineering. EZ-NP delivers reliable, eco-conscious preservation for organizations dedicated to protecting the natural world.

EZ-OU: Beneath the Waves, Beyond the Horizon.
Built for the deepest oceans of 2125, EZ-OU is EasyCorp's stingray-inspired exploration unit. Its fluid shape and adaptive skin let it blend seamlessly with marine life, gathering data without disrupting natural behavior.Powered by hydro-quantum thrusters and protected by abyss-grade materials, EZ-OU operates where standard submersibles cannot. Its multi-spectrum sensors capture high-detail environmental and behavioral data for research and conservation. Quiet, resilient, and intelligent, EZ-OU offers a gentle yet advanced approach to deep-ocean discovery.
